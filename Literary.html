Club Coordinator

The club offers a platform for the students to exhibit their atheistic talents. It promises to cultivate a passion and enthusiasm for quizzing, debating, declaiming, creative writing, and art among the students. The club has been instrumental in building a community of creative, imaginative and thoughtful students. The students have been greatly benefitted by their performance and participation in literary events.

Literary Club aims to:

  • Enhance listening, reading, speaking, and writing (LSWR) skills of students.
  • Enrich the general knowledge of students.
  • Develop soft skills such as effective communication, leadership skills, and interpersonal communication skills.
  • To teach students to think spontaneously.

Each semester the club organizes a rich and diverse array of literary activities such as debates, declamations, group discussions, poetry writing, and documentary race. The ‘Declamation Competition’ is now an annual event in the college. Through these activities, literary coordinators (student and teacher) reach out to language and literature lovers and try to create a space for sharing emotions and thoughts.
